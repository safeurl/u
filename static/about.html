About

The benefits of using a URL shortener service to share URLs are well known. But what many URL shortener services do under the guise of free is sell your and your user's data to 3rd party trackers. We built this service, not only to offer a privacy-oriented option but also one which protects your URL from spambots. eg. WhatsApp/Telegram group chat links are often a target of spambots in online forums.

Captcha

The current approach is to challenge users with a captcha before they can see the original URL. Yes, we hate captcha too. Given how URL shortener services are especially prone to abuse, captcha offers the right balance between security and user experience. It is very hard to differentiate between a spambot and a legitimate user without harvesting the user's activity data. The choice between violating user's privacy and an extra step is a simple one. You can also install Privacy Pass extension to bypass the captcha challenge while preserving user anonymity.

We rely on hCaptcha for the captcha challenge. hCaptcha is a popular privacy-focused alternative to Google's reCAPTCHA and is trusted by large internet companies like Cloudflare.

Of course, captcha is not a complete solution for spam in any way. There are many businesses that employ an army of Humans to solve captchas. The goal is to make it difficult enough so that it is not economically viable to spammers.

Also, everytime you solve a captcha, you make a small donation to the Wikimedia Foundation.



Security

To protect our users from Malware or Phishing, we do not auto-redirect users to the original URL. The users are shown the full URL in text and make a conscious decision on whether to visit the full URL or not.
