Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected and used when you visit https://okurl.in/ (the “Site”).

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT


When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device - information about your web browser, IP address, time zone. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Device Information.”

When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are ONLY talking about Device Information.

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

- Server access logs when you visit the Site, this includes your IP address, browser type, and date/time stamps.

This information is required to ban abusing IP addresses and to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

There are NO Cookies for cross-site tracking or identifying visitors.
We use Cloudflare to manage web traffic and detect malicious visitors. For information on how Cloudflare collects and processes data, please click here.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?


The device information is used to show statistics on short URLs. This information is public for everyone. The information is anonymised and not shown until a minimum threshold count is reached. We set that minimum threshold to 5. Individual device information is never reported.

SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION


We DO NOT share your Personal Information with third parties.

We may share Device Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

DATA RETENTION


The Server logs are only stored for last 3 months.

CHANGES


We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

CONTACT US


For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at privacy@r4v1.net.
